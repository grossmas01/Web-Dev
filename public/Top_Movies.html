The Best Movies According to Susie

My Top 3 Movies of all Times

Weird Science

Old school 80's comedy starring Kelly LeBrock as the woman that two high school geeks created.

Rocket Man

Disney comedy about a geeky aeronautics engineer who by a stroke of luck, or in his case, by his own accident-proness, becomes an astronaut.

Tombstone

Modern western starring Kirk Russell and Val Kilmer. In his greatest roll as Doc Holliday, Val Kilmer steals the show in this high-tension drama.
